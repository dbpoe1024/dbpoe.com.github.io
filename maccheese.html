Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs macaroni
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 gallon milk
  • 8 cups shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes
  3. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes making sure the milk does not burn.
  6. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.
  7. Fold the drained macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.
