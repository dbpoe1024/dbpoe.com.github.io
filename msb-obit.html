Michelle Susan Brenneman

April 21, 1962 - April 24, 2024

Michelle Susan Brenneman passed away in Albuquerque, NM on Wednesday, April 24, 2024 at the age of 62.

She was born on April 21, 1962 in Lubbock, TX and raised in Phoenix, AZ.

Michelle is survived by her siblings, Holly Brown and Jayson Featherstone; her children, Eunoia Green, Stephanie Lee, Rebekah Rios, and David Poe; and four grandchildren. She was preceded in death by her parents, John Walker and Patsy Walker; and brother, Michael Walker.
