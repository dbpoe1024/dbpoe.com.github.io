Michelle Susan Brenneman

April 21, 1962 - April 24, 2024

Michelle Susan Brenneman passed away in Albuquerque, NM on Wednesday, April 24, 2024 at the age of 62.

She was born on April 21, 1962 in Lubbock, TX and grew up in Phoenix, AZ.

Michelle is survived by her children, Eunoia Green, Stephanie Lee, Rebekah Rios, and David Poe; four grandchildren; and her siblings, Holly Brown and Jayson Featherstone. She was preceded in death by her parents, John Walker and Patsy Thompson; and brother, Michael Walker.
