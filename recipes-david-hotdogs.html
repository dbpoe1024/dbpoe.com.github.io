David's Bacon-Wrapped Hot Dogs 🌭

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder, optional
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder, optional
  • Preferred toppings, condiments, and garnishes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â° and line a rimmed baking sheet with foil.
  2. Cut shallow, diagonal slits into each hot dog about 1 inch apart. Wrap one piece of bacon around each hot dog. Sprinkle both sides of the bacon-wrapped hot dog with garlic powder and chili powder, if you like.
  3. Place the bacon-wrapped hot dogs in the baking sheet and bake until the bacon is crispy, 20 to 25 minutes, turning them over halfway through the baking time.
  4. Add the bacon-wrapped hot dogs to the buns and add your toppings, condiments, and garnishes.
